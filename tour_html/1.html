Blood Pressure: Follow your doctor's instructions for when and how often to check your blood pressure.

Factors to be aware of, Blood pressure normally rise as a result of:

  • Stress
  • Cold temperatures
  • Smoking (Nicotine)
  • Certain medication
  • Vigorous Exercise
  • Caffeine

Make sure that when testing your blood pressure you are completely relaxed; read the manual on your blood pressure machine for further testing instructions.
