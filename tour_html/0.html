Master Readings

Chronic Lifestyle Diseases such as High Blood Pressure, Obesity and Diabetes are among the leading cause of death and concern globally. These diseases can be prevented and or avoided once detected early. MEE Time gives users the opportunity to monitor their medical and health conditions regularly so that the effects of these diseases can be controlled. For the first time, users will be able to analyze key medical readings and their responses to lifestyle habits. These insights will be an invaluable tool for your wellness goals and can be emailed to your medical practitioner, enabling him to see a true holistic picture of your readings over time.
